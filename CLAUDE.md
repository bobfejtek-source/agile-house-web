# Agile House - AI-First Digital Studio

## What is this
Landing page and web presence for Agile House s.r.o., a Czech-based AI-first digital studio. The website serves as both a portfolio and a lead generation tool for potential clients and partners.

## Who we are
Family-run AI studio. Bohdan (founder/CEO) brings McKinsey and UBS transformation background. Wife and son are part of the team. We operate lean by design - AI agents handle the work of entire departments, human judgment drives every strategic decision. Small team = no bureaucracy, no account managers, direct access to the people who build.

## Design system
- **Fonts**: Instrument Serif (headlines, italic labels), Outfit (body, weight 300/400/500), JetBrains Mono (code, terminal, tags)
- **Accent color**: #D85A30 (coral)
- **Background**: dark, transitions from #0A0A08 (black) to dark amber on scroll
- **Effects**: Matrix code rain (amber tinted), floating AI prompt snippets, pipeline tracker on right, terminal animations, typing prompt in hero
- **Layout**: centered, max-width container, responsive
